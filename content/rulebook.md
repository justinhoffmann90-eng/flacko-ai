## Indicator Quick Reference

### BX-Trender
Four distinct states based on structure:
- **Light Green (HH)** = Strong bullish - making higher highs
- **Dark Green (LH)** = Weakening bullish - no longer making new highs (warning)
- **Light Red (HL)** = Weakening bearish - potential upside turnaround
- **Dark Red (LL)** = Strong bearish - making lower lows

**Key insight:** The signal is in the TRANSITION. Dark Red â†’ Light Red is bullish. Light Green â†’ Dark Green is a warning.

### SMI (Stochastic Momentum Index)
- Oversold (<-40) = bounce potential
- Overbought (>+40) = pullback risk
- Bullish cross = momentum turning up
- Bearish cross = momentum turning down

### Weekly EMAs (9/13/21)
- 9 EMA = Momentum
- 13 EMA = Control
- 21 EMA = Structure (last line of defense)
- Above all with 9>13>21 = healthy trend
