| **Trim Cap** | How much you trim at resistance | % of remaining position |

## How Daily Cap Works

**Daily cap = % of your available cash, not your portfolio.**

**Example: YELLOW mode (17.5% cap)**
- Portfolio: $100,000
- Current TSLA position: $40,000
- Available cash: $60,000
- Daily cap: $60,000 Ã— 17.5% = **$10,500**

**The key insight:** As you deploy cash, your daily cap in dollar terms shrinks. This naturally paces your entries.

---

## How Max Invested Works

**Max invested = % of your total portfolio in TSLA positions.**

**Example: ORANGE mode (40% max)**
- Portfolio: $100,000
- Max TSLA position: $40,000
- Current position: $35,000
- Room to add: $5,000

**Even if you have cash and daily cap available**, you can't exceed max invested. This prevents overexposure in deteriorating conditions.

## Position Sizing Framework

### Step 1: Check Your Mode
Look at today's report for the current mode.

### Step 2: Calculate Daily Cap
```
Daily Cap = Available Cash Ã— Mode %
```
If Slow Zone active, halve the result.

### Step 3: Check Max Invested
```
Room to Add = (Portfolio Ã— Max Invested %) - Current Position
```
Your actual cap is the LOWER of daily cap and room to add.

### Step 4: Determine Conviction
| Conviction | % of Cap to Use |
|------------|-----------------|
| High (strong setup, multiple confirmations) | 75-100% |
| Medium (decent setup, some confluence) | 50-75% |
| Low (speculative, single signal) | 25-50% |

### Step 5: Calculate Position
```
Position Size = Daily Cap Ã— Conviction %
```

## Scaling In

Don't deploy your full cap in one trade. Scale in:

| Entry | % of Position | When |
|-------|---------------|------|
| First | 40-50% | Initial signal, level touches |
| Second | 30-40% | Confirmation, level holds |
| Third | 20-30% | Strength, breakout confirmed |

**Why scale?**
- Reduces bad entry risk
- Improves average cost
- Lets you react to new information
- Keeps cash available for better setups

---

## When to Size Down

Even within your cap, size down for:

| Situation | Use This Much of Cap |
|-----------|---------------------|
| Late to the move | 50% |
| Conflicting signals | 50% |
| Near resistance | 50% |
| Multiple open positions | 50-75% |
| High VIX (>25) | 50-75% |
| First position after EJECTED | 50% |

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Ignoring max invested | Check BEFORE deploying |
| Same size every trade | Adjust for mode + conviction |
| Ignoring Slow Zone | Halve cap when active |
| Going all-in on first entry | Scale in 3 entries |
| No cash reserve | Always keep some available |

| Principle | Remember |
