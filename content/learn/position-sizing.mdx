## Why position sizing matters more than entries

Most traders obsess over entries. "Where do I buy?" 

The better question: **"How much do I buy?"**

A great entry with wrong sizing can still blow up your account. A mediocre entry with proper sizing keeps you in the game.

## Cap is a ceiling, not a target

**Important**: The daily cap is your *maximum*, not your required size.

- Green mode with 25% cap doesn't mean you *must* deploy 25%
- It means you *can* deploy up to 25% if the setup is right
- No setup? Deploy nothing. That's fine.

The goal isn't to never lose. It's to **lose small** so you can keep playing.

## Scaling in

You don't have to deploy your full cap at once. Scaling in works:

1. **Initial position**: 30-50% of your daily cap
2. **Add on confirmation**: If the move works, add more
3. **Full position**: Only if thesis is confirmed and structure holds

This way, your average entry is better if you're right, and your loss is smaller if you're wrong.
