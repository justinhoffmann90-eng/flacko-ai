## Calculation Framework

### Step 1: Calculate Daily Cap
`Daily Buy Capacity = Available Cash Ã— Mode Daily Cap`

### Step 2: Calculate Max Room
`Max Room = (Portfolio Ã— Max Invested Cap) - Current Exposure`

### Step 3: Use the Lower Number
Your actionable buy size is the lower of:
- daily buy capacity, or
- max room

## Max Invested Caps Are Tiered Guidance

Mode downgrades are managed progressively in most cases.

| Transition | Guidance |

| ORANGE â†’ RED | Strong suggestion to work toward ~20-30% |

**Key point:** Except EJECTED rules, max invested caps are primarily **risk guidance**, not auto-liquidation triggers.

## Key Takeaway

Correct sizing is a 3-part check:
1. Mode cap
2. Slow Zone adjustment
3. Max invested room

If all three align, you deploy. If not, you wait.
