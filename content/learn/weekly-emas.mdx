## What Are Weekly EMAs?

EMAs (Exponential Moving Averages) smooth price action and reveal **trend direction**. We use three on the weekly chart:

| EMA | Period | Role |
|-----|--------|------|
| **9 EMA** | Short-term | Momentum, first warning |
| **13 EMA** | Medium-term | Trend confirmation |
| **21 EMA** | Long-term | Major support/resistance |

## EMA Signals

| Signal | What It Means | Action |
|--------|---------------|--------|
| **Price above all EMAs** | Uptrend intact | Lean bullish |
| **Price below all EMAs** | Downtrend intact | Lean bearish |
| **Price between EMAs** | Transition/choppy | Reduce size |
| **9 crosses 13** | Momentum shift | Watch for confirmation |
| **Price touches 21** | Key support/resistance | High-probability reaction zone |
