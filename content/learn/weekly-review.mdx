## What gets tracked

### Level Performance
Every key level from every daily report:
- Did the Put Wall hold as support?
- Did the Call Wall act as resistance?
- Was the Gamma Strike pivot respected?

### Scenario Accuracy
Each day has Bull/Base/Bear scenarios. We track:
- Which scenario triggered?
- Did the expected target get hit?
- Was the action guidance correct?

### Mode Appropriateness  
Looking back at the week:
- Were green modes actually good for offense?
- Did red modes correctly identify danger?
- Would different sizing have helped or hurt?

## Learning from accuracy data

The weekly review isn't just a report card. It's a learning tool.

**Patterns emerge over time:**
- Which levels are most reliable?
- When do scenarios tend to miss?
- What market conditions make predictions harder?

**Adjustments follow:**
- Weight reliable levels more heavily
- Size down when conditions are historically tricky
- Trust the process when it's been working
