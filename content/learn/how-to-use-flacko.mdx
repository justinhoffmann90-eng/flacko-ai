## The System at a Glance

Flacko AI is a **risk-first trading system** for TSLA.

| Component | What You Get | Frequency |
|-----------|--------------|-----------|
| **Daily Report** | Mode, levels, scenarios, action plan | Every morning |
| **Price Alerts** | Notifications at key levels | Real-time |
| **Weekly Review** | Performance analysis | Every week |

**Goal:** Know your risk before the open. React to levels with a plan.
