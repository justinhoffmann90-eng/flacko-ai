## What Are Alerts?

Alerts notify you when price hits a **key level**. Each alert includes:

| Component | What It Tells You |
|-----------|-------------------|
| **Level** | The price that triggered (e.g., $420) |
| **Name** | What the level is (e.g., Gamma Strike) |
| **Action** | What to consider doing |
| **Context** | Why this level matters |
