## What's In the Daily Report?

The report drops **pre-market** and gives you everything you need for the day.

---

## Report Structure

| Section | What It Contains | Time to Read |
|---------|------------------|--------------|
| **Mode** | Today's risk level and cap | 10 seconds |
| **Key Levels** | Gamma Strike, Call Wall, Put Wall, etc. | 30 seconds |
| **Scenarios** | Bull, Base, Bear probabilities | 1 minute |
| **SpotGamma Data** | Dealer positioning, gamma profile | 1 minute |
| **Technicals** | EMAs, trend structure | 30 seconds |
| **Action Plan** | What to do at each level | 1 minute |

**Total time: ~4 minutes**

## Pro Tips

| Tip | Why It Matters |
|-----|----------------|
| **Read it before 9:30 AM** | Levels can change at open |
| **Screenshot key levels** | Quick reference during session |
| **Re-read at lunch** | Context can shift mid-day |
| **Compare to yesterday** | Did levels move significantly? |
