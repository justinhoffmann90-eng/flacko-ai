## Understanding the Caps

### Daily Cap
How much of your **available cash** you can deploy in a single day.

**Example:** GREEN mode with $100,000 cash = $30,000 max deployment today.

**Why it matters:** Caps force you to spread entries over multiple days. In YELLOW, you need 5-6 days to reach max invested. This protects you from going all-in before a correction deepens.

### Max Invested
The maximum percentage of your portfolio that should be in TSLA positions.

**Example:** ORANGE mode = 40% max invested. If you have $200,000, max $80,000 in TSLA.

**Why it matters:** Even if you have cash and daily cap available, you shouldn't exceed your max invested limit for the current mode. This prevents overexposure when conditions are deteriorating.

### Trim Cap
How much of your **remaining position** to trim at each resistance level.

**Example:** ORANGE mode = 25% trim cap. If you hold $40,000 in TSLA and hit T1, trim $10,000.

**Why it matters:** In defensive modes, bounces are suspect. Higher trim caps help you lock in gains faster. In GREEN, you let winners run with smaller trims.

## YELLOW Improving vs Standard YELLOW

**YELLOW (Improving)** is a submode that triggers when the Daily BX-Trender is 2+ stages ahead of Weekly in a recovery pattern.

| Submode | Trigger | Daily Cap | Why |
|---------|---------|-----------|-----|
| YELLOW (Improving) | Daily green while Weekly still red | 20% | Recovery underway, accumulate meaningfully |
| YELLOW (Standard) | Warning signs, mixed structure | 17.5% | Uncertain, spread entries more |

**Key insight:** YELLOW Improving lets you build positions faster during early recoveries, while standard YELLOW keeps you cautious during deterioration.

## The EJECTED State

When price closes below the Weekly 21 EMA for **two consecutive days**, you enter the EJECTED state:

1. **Sell ALL leverage** (TSLL, options) immediately ‚Äî non-negotiable
2. **Trim TSLA to 50%** of portfolio ‚Äî hold core shares
3. **Daily cap drops to 0%** ‚Äî no new buys via normal rules
4. **200 SMA Oversold Override activates** ‚Äî only way to deploy in deeply oversold conditions

**Why partial exit, not full liquidation?** The W21 EMA break signals structure damage, but the bull thesis may still be intact. You protect capital by cutting leverage and trimming, but you keep 50% core exposure for the recovery.

## The Slow Zone Modifier

When price closes below Daily 21 EMA √ó 0.98 (2% below), the **Slow Zone** activates:

- **Effect:** Halve your daily cap
- **Example:** GREEN 30% becomes 15% while Slow Zone active
- **Resume:** When price reclaims Daily 21 EMA
- **Exception:** Inactive in GREEN and YELLOW (Improving) modes

The Slow Zone creates a graduated response between normal accumulation and Master Eject. It slows you down without stopping you entirely.
