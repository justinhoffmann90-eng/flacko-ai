## Important Label Notes

- **YELLOW (Improving)** is a valid documented state.
- **ORANGE (Improving)** is also valid and can appear when **Tier 1 (Weekly) is LL** and **Tier 2 (Daily) is HL**.

These improving labels mean internals are stabilizing, even if the broader structure is still defensive.

## Understanding the Caps

### Daily Buy Cap
How much of your **available cash** you can deploy in one day.

### Max Invested Cap
The maximum share of your **total portfolio** that can be in TSLA exposure for that mode.

### Trim Cap
How much of your **remaining position** to trim at each T-level.

## EJECTED Is a Defense State, Not Full Liquidation

When Weekly 21 EMA breaks and confirms:
1. Cut all leverage first
2. Trim TSLA to 50%
3. Keep core exposure
4. Only add through oversold override rules

This is graduated defense, not panic liquidation.
