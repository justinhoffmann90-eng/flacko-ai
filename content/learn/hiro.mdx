## Reading the number

HIRO is expressed in dollar terms (millions/billions):

| Reading | Interpretation |
|---------|----------------|
| **+$500M or higher** | Bullish â€” dealers actively buying to hedge |
| **+$100M to +$500M** | Leaning bullish â€” mild accumulation |
| **-$100M to +$100M** | Neutral â€” no strong directional pressure |
| **-$100M to -$500M** | Leaning bearish â€” mild distribution |
| **-$500M or lower** | Bearish â€” dealers actively selling |

## HIRO vs price action

HIRO tells you what dealers are doing. Price tells you what already happened.

**Confirmation**: HIRO and price moving together. Strong signal.
- Price up + HIRO bullish = real buying, trend supported
- Price down + HIRO bearish = real selling, weakness confirmed

**Divergence**: HIRO and price disagreeing. Watch for reversals.
- Price up + HIRO flat/negative = rally lacks support, caution
- Price down + HIRO improving = selling exhaustion, watch for bounce

## How we use HIRO

HIRO is one input into the **mode** calculation. It doesn't override levels, but it adds crucial context:

- **Strong HIRO + good levels** = confidence to size up
- **Weak HIRO + good levels** = still trade, but respect the caution
- **Strong HIRO + bad levels** = flow is supportive but structure says wait
- **Weak HIRO + bad levels** = stay defensive
